ABOUT PERUVIAN CUISINE

Peruvian cuisine is a reflection of Peru's diverse geography and rich cultural heritage. It blends indigenous ingredients with Spanish, African, Chinese, and Japanese influences, creating one of the world's most unique and celebrated culinary traditions.

The three main regions: coastal, Andean, and Amazonian. Each contribute distinct flavors, ingredients, and cooking techniques that have been perfected over centuries.
